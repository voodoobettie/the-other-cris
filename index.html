Estancia Ongamira is a 1300 acre rural farm situated in one of the most beautiful valleys of the Cordoba hills, Argentina. We are a family-run cattle farm. We produce the finest Argentine beef because we have the best cattle. Our herd is tended to with humane treatment and reared on a grass-fed diet.

Since 1926 Estancia Ongamira has been a family owned and operated business.

Open all year round, Estancia Ongamira is the perfect place to get away from it all without losing touch with the modern world. We offer our guests the standards of service and attention to detail you would expect of an international hotel.

Estancia Ongamira is located 120 Km North of the City of Cordoba and the Cordoba international / domestic airport. Transfers available upon request.

History

The origins of Estancia Ongamira date back to the late 16th Century, founded by the Spanish Conquistadores, who by orders of the King of Spain built the Chapel of St Joseph to pacify and evangelize the Comechingones tribes of the area.

FAQ

Are you an organic farm?

No, we aren't an organic farm for the simple reason that it would prevent us from helping our sick animals. Cattle sometimes kick one another when we bring them into the corrales and infection is common so we use antibiotics on injured animals rather than let them die from a treatable injury. Except when we bring them in, the cattle are free to roam our ample pastures and graze together on the grasses of the Argentine Pampas.

Weather Information

Ongamira Valley has a micro climate, quite mild all year round, and plenty of rainfall. Summer daytime temperatures are pleasant and reach about 30°C / 85°F, overnight temperatures are cooler. In Fall and Winter the daytime temperatures can get as high as a pleasant 23°C / 74°F, however in the evenings it can get quite cold. Occasionally we get some light snowfalls, in June or July. We are at an altitude of 3,600 feet above sea level.
