Privacy and Security

 

At Estancia Ongamira, the privacy and security of our distinguished guests are our top priority. VIP guests find our facilities to be very private, relaxing, anonymous and secure. As a VIP guest we will use utmost discretion regarding your visit.

Exclusive use: Upon request, arrangements can be made for you to have exclusive use of the premises. In these circumstances, our staff will not be made aware of the special arrangements until shortly before your arrival in order to maintain secrecy, and only essential personnel will be on the premises during your stay. No other guests will be allowed during your stay.

Security: We are able to provide a 24-hour armed guard, if desired. Generally for this we use off-duty police officers who will patrol the premises. We have the ability to totally lock down the estancia for you.

Concierge: We can provide you with transportation at any time you require, and any other arrangements you may need during your stay with us. We have multilingual staff available at all times, so you need not be concerned about language barriers.

Should you be interested in these services, please advise us at the time of booking your holiday with us.
