Activities

We offer many activities both in and around the Estancia, including:

  • Horseback riding through the valley and surrounding hills
  • Bird watching
  • Swimming in our mineral spring fed pool
  • Hiking and walking tours
  • Golf available nearby - please book early
  • River Trout fishing and guided tours at the Tramontana Hatchery near Capilla del Monte
  • Para-Gliding in Cuchi-Corral (near La Cumbre)

Tours

Estancia Ongamira is not far from the picturesque towns of Los Cocos, La Cumbre and Capilla Del Monte, and the Fernando Fader Museum, on the way to the quaint old village of Ischilin Viejo (founded in the 1700). Other great places to visit are the World Heritage listed Jesuit Monasteries. The closest to us is the Monastery of Santa Catalina, which dates back to the early 1600s and provides a fascinating look at local history. They all have guided tours available on-site. We highly recommend a visit.
