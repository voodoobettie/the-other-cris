Welcome to the rural heart of Argentina

Relax and enjoy the serenity of the Estancia Ongamira, whether you're on horseback, relaxing by our pool or enjoying the garden, we know that you will love the spectacular countryside we have to offer.

Our hotel is situated in the Ongamira Valley, which is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful valleys in the Cordoba hills, the air is clean and the water is fresh from a mountain spring. Open all year round, the Estancia is the perfect place to get away from it all without losing touch with the modern world. We offer high-speed internet as well as fax and phone facilities and we're conveniently located only 120 Km North of the City of Cordoba and the Cordoba international / domestic airport. Airport transfers available upon request.

In the evenings you can enjoy music and drinks in our Pub after enjoying a fine meal. Our cuisine is refined traditional country style but our chef Jean is able to cook western homestyle meals if desired.

We speak fluent English, Spanish, French and Italian.
