Dining in Style
   

Meal times are always a special occasion at Estancia Ongamira. We use the finest local ingredients and a wide variety of organic fruits, vegetables and herbs grown on our farm, depending on seasonal availability.

Expect the Unexpected

Our cuisine ranges from traditional Argentine asado (barbeque) through to Asian-inspired dishes and upon special request we can prepare vegetarian and celiac friendly meals.

We have a well stocked bar, with both Argentine and imported liquor, so you are sure to find your favourite brands.

While you dine you can admire the original calicanto (stone and lime) wall and archway, part of the Chapel of St Joseph built in 1597 by the Spaniards as part of the effort to colonize the Native Indian tribes of the region.
