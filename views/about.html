About Us   Our Staff

The origins of Estancia Ongamira date back to the late XVIth Century. The building we use as our dining room today was built as a Chapel in 1597 by the Spanish Conquistador, Captain Juan de Burgos Celiz.
Estancia Ongamira is a family owned and operated business, which began as a cattle ranch and family retreat in 1926, The hotel business is a new venture that commenced operations in late 2005. We have followed organic practices for over 30 years.

Estancia Ongamira is now a luxurious Hotel Estancia which offers all the conveniences of modern living such as high-speed internet access, telephone, fax, satellite broadcast television, pool, bar and restaurant with international cuisine. You can get away from it all and still stay in touch with the world while you relax and enjoy a well deserved holiday.
Ongamira Valley is a beautiful mountain retreat, with clean air and mineral spring water.

We know that you will be delighted with your stay here because we wouldn't have it any other way.
